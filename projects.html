Projects

Computational Biology Research Assistant

I am working with Dr. Brian Chen and Ph.D. candidate Ziyi Guo at Lehigh University to develop VASP, volumetric alignment software applied to bone structures and electrostatic isopotentials of proteins.

Catapult Entrepreneurship Incubator

I worked on a team of six to develop an early-stage startup called Vocap, an app that uses entertainment to teach foreign language vocabulary. Over the course of four and months and under the mentorship of Wharton Business School students, I gained hands-on experience with the Lean Startup model, branding, market research, and storytelling.

Mini Globetrotters

During high school, I was co-director of an organization called Mini Globetrotters, a student-led community service club designed to spread multiculturalism to elementary students in the Lehigh Valley. I created a website for the organization from scratch and designed the logo using Adobe Illustrator.

MIT Blueprint Hackathon

Over the course of 12 hours, I worked on a team of three to develop Halp, a website that connects high school students to college mentors. We used HTML/CSS to design the website and used Javascript to query a database set up with Parse. Our project won a Second Place award.
